ABOUT

Peter is the Executive Chef of Le Mensch and its sister restaurant La Femme, both situated at the edge of Sydney Harbour.
Peter’s creative and original cuisine has seen both restaurants receive an unprecedented number of Australian and international awards. Peter continues to be
a leading figure in Australia’s vibrant food scene.

EVENTS

Located in the iconic Sydney Opera House, Le Mensch is a truly unique event venue; a pinnacle of Sydney’s premier dining experiences. The internal architecture of The House, Circular Quay and the Royal Botanic Gardens provide a spectacular backdrop for an impressive event centre stage Sydney Harbour.
